Services Overview

Technical Education forms the backbone of development of a nation. Industry is the major consumer of technical institution product. The real requirement of industry will be explored only through effective interaction with them. Interaction between the industry and institute is now widely recognized. Through this forum institutions, individuals and industries can come closer to each other and have barrier free interaction to understand each other’s requirement, so that suitably trained, fully equipped with need based skills, manpower can be supplied by technical institution to the industries.
India is blessed with population of about 70% below the age of 35 years and youths are the most vibrant and dynamic segment as well as potentially most viable human recourse. At same time India is seriously handicapped with very weak and narrow knowledge base with 12.3% gross enrollment ratio as compared to 21% in China and 54.6% in developed countries and the world average is 23.2%. There is a need to convert the available huge human resource potential into a reality by expanding opportunities in the fields such as science technology, engineering, architecture, management etc.
